about the service
this service was created by christian schäl, a german it student. this projects main motivation originates from watching a multiplayer game recorded by different players from different viewpoints as a single video. instead of viewing the video in 4 different windows next to each other and struggling with correct timing, performance and synced playback, you simply submit videos and stream the combined output on any device.
